Assistiv strategic assessment · June 2026
The market

A problem the system
cannot ignore for much longer.

The UK's ageing population is not a future problem it is a present crisis. Emergency admissions for older people are rising faster than NHS capacity. Social care waiting lists are at record lengths. The cost of delayed intervention is measured in billions. Assistiv addresses the upstream cause: 3.5 million people who fall between the gaps in the current system.

The addressable market spans three revenue streams across NHS commissioning, individual and family subscriptions, and commercial partnerships with care and security providers. The platform is architected to serve all three simultaneously from a single infrastructure creating compounding defensibility as each stream grows.

🏥
Model A Commercial partnership
Passive safety monitoring and ARC-integrated fall detection, white-labelled or co-branded with a strategic security partner. Recurring per-home licence revenue. Low sales cycle. Strong ROI case for the partner.
👨‍👩‍👧
Model B Individual subscription
Full platform sensing, Inner Circle, Unified Care Record, ARC delivered direct to older people and their families. Monthly subscription, hardware included. The primary IP asset and long-term revenue engine.
📊
Model C NHS commissioning
Population intelligence and virtual ward integration, commissioned by ICBs and PCNs. assistiv.cloud is being prepared for commissioner engagement via NHS Health Innovation KSS and the Kent & Medway commissioner network. The 14:1 modelled ROI makes the commissioning case compelling at pilot scale.
🔒
Strategic security partner route
An established security and monitoring partner brings existing ARC infrastructure, trusted installer networks, and a customer base that maps directly onto the Assistiv target population. Model A is the entry point. Models B and C follow.
The roadmap

Four milestones to
seed round readiness.

Four concrete proof points will transform the investment narrative from compelling to closed. Each milestone builds on the last and together they represent the foundation on which commercial scale is built.

01
Signed commercial agreement or letter of intent
A signed LOI or paid pilot agreement even at modest value transforms "commercial interest exists" into "commercial validation secured." This is the single most important near-term task. A Model A pilot changes the valuation conversation significantly.
Target: 3–6 months
02
One home pilot deployment sensors live
A single household with sensors installed, data flowing and a consented resident. This is the difference between concept and proof of concept. An established partner with installer networks makes this achievable in months, not years.
Target: 4–8 months
03
Senior engineering hire with hardware and embedded systems experience
With Paul Schelhaas as CINO owning technology strategy, a senior hardware engineer supporting on firmware, LoRaWAN integration and edge processing gives the platform its first full technical delivery team. Likely equity-led at this stage.
Target: 3–5 months
04
Clinical validation pathway letter of intent from a GP practice or PCN
Not the study itself the agreement to do one. A letter of intent from a Kent GP practice, PCN or hospital trust to participate in a feasibility study gives investors a clinical validation pathway to assess. Professor Jerrim's route into Kent trust clinical leadership is the priority path.
Target: 4–6 months

Strategic partnership

What the right partner
brings to the table.

The Assistiv platform is designed to be deployed through a partner with existing ARC infrastructure, installer networks, and a trusted customer base. Assistiv provides the intelligence and protocol. The partner provides the operational reach that turns proof of concept into commercial scale.

🏗
ARC infrastructure
An existing, staffed Alarm Receiving Centre provides the 24/7 response capability the Assistiv Response Centre requires without the cost and time of building one. This is years of infrastructure, available immediately.
🔧
Installer networks
Trained installation teams who already enter homes, already have insurance and compliance frameworks, and already have relationships with the property sector and social housing. The home pilot deployment milestone becomes a matter of weeks, not months.
👥
Existing customer base
A security and monitoring partner's existing customers include the exact population Assistiv targets older people, families and local authorities already paying for monitoring services. Model A is a product upgrade for customers they already hold.
