How this is scored

Each of the eight scored domains carries a preference weight for the answer chosen. The weights are summed and converted with the ASCOT-SCT4 formula SCRQoL = 0.203 × weighted score − 0.466, giving a figure anchored to the ideal state at 1.00. The dignity filter question is asked but not scored, as per the ASCOT guidance. Weighting and thresholds follow the ASCOT-SCT4 guidance (Version 2.0, September 2025).
